About

I'm a math/science guy who turned out to have a penchant for both creating video content and spotting what's coming next well ahead of the curve.

More recently, I've gone beyond video (TV, social, B2B) to include photo, logos, AI integration, digital experiences, and whatever else the moment demands.

If that doesn't qualify me as a generalist, then my portfolio certainly does.

I've filmed sharks for Shark Week, puppies for Puppy Bowl, A-list celebrities for media junkets, heads of state for PSAs, world-class athletes for international broadcasts—and that's just the start. While the content has been wide-ranging, there's been one constant: the push for what comes next.

I helped define several major corporate rebrands as Discovery evolved from a small basic cable network to a Top 3 media company. I launched marketing for discovery+, Food Network Kitchen, and Discovery VR as we pioneered new formats and took up arms in the streaming wars. I developed a show for gamers just as Amazon was acquiring Twitch. I designed an on-demand model for live TV on TBS before VOD was a thing. And now I'm integrating AI into creative workflows, elevating quality, finding efficiency, and feeding my curiosity every day.

If you're working on something different, complex, or unproven, I'm the guy who can lead a team to figure it out, craft the message, and deliver results that actually move numbers.

Turns out I have a knack for being in "the room where it happens." And in this ever-changing world of content and marketing, that's a pretty good skill to have.
